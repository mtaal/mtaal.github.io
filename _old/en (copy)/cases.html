Customers

Heijmans

Heijmans is one of the largest building companies in The Netherlands with a turnover of 2,6 billion euros. Springsite delivers a web-based procurement system which gives Heijmans visibility of supplier performance and administrative information. The software supports the quotation process and allows purchasers to identify shared procurement opportunities.

Supplier management
  • Central supplier management system with >13000 suppliers
  • Supplier classification by worktype
  • Company wide access to detailed supplier information
Quote process, Supplier assessment, procurement planning
  • Project based supplier selection, supports quotation process
  • Quote response registration, quote/contract scoring
  • Supplier assessment, company wide access to supplier scores
  • Identify shared procurement opportunities

Cordeo

Cordeo offers a printing-on-demand solution which streamlines procurement in marketing oriented organisations. To support this process Springsite delivers an eProcurement platform for printing-on-demand solutions.

eProcurement Platform
  • Ordering system with product catalogs
  • Budget/cost oriented approval work flow
  • Integrates with suppliers through xml with email/ftp
  • Hierarchical price lists
  • Multi-currency, multi-locale

Yageo

Online product database

  • Product Modeling, System Integration
Parametric search
  • Adaptive web dialogs for product search using technical product attributes
  • Efficient in-memory search structures

    • www.yageo.com/parametric
Competitor cross reference
